# 📦 FMCG Mart Supply Chain Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This is an end-to-end Supply Chain Analytics project in the FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) domain.

The objective of this project is to solve a real-world business problem by building an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides actionable insights to stakeholders and enables data-driven decision-making.

## 🧩 Business Problem

AtliQ Mart, a growing FMCG manufacturer headquartered in Gujarat, India, operates across three major cities:

    Surat
    
    Ahmedabad
    
    Vadodara

As part of its expansion strategy into Tier-1 cities, the company encountered a critical challenge:

👉 Several key customers did not renew their annual contracts due to poor service levels.

The root cause was identified as:
    
    Delays in product deliveries
    
    Incomplete order fulfillment over a prolonged period
    
    This directly impacted customer satisfaction and trust.

--- 

## 🎯 Objective

Before expanding further, management aimed to:

    Monitor delivery performance on a daily basis
    
    Track service-level KPIs to identify issues early
    
    Improve overall supply chain efficiency
    
---

## 📌 Key Metrics Tracked

The Supply Chain team adopted industry-standard KPIs:

    OT % (On-Time Delivery)
    
    IF % (In-Full Delivery)
    
    OTIF % (On-Time In-Full Delivery)
    
    LIFR % (Line Fill Rate)
    
    VOFR % (Volume Fill Rate)

👉 All metrics are tracked against target service levels to measure performance gaps.

## 💡 Key Insights

🥛 Dairy category dominates overall order volume

📉 All key KPIs (OT%, IF%, OTIF%) are below target, indicating poor supply chain performance

🚚 Incomplete deliveries are a major issue, reflected in low IF%

📦 Line Fill Rate (~66%) shows many orders are not fulfilled completely

📊 Volume Fill Rate (~96%) indicates minor quantity gaps but consistent shortfalls

📉 No significant improvement trend observed over recent months

⚠️ Large gap between IF% and target, possibly due to production or inventory issues

🏪 High-order customers like Lotus Mart, Coolblue, and Acclaimed Stores also experience frequent delays

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

Improve inventory planning to reduce stockouts

Optimize logistics and delivery routes

Focus on high-volume, low-performance customers

Implement real-time monitoring for faster issue resolution

---

## 📈 Business Impact

This dashboard enables:

    Better visibility into service-level performance
    
    Faster identification of operational bottlenecks
    
    Improved customer satisfaction and retention
    
    Data-driven decision-making for expansion strategy
